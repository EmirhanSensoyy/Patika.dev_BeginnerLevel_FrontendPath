TRILECHE RECIPE

Directions

Although the origin of trileche, one of the most popular desserts in the world, is unknown, it is in Mexico. It is said to be widespread. The name of this dessert whose main ingredients are sponge cake and milk, is It means '3 milks' in Spanish. trilecheusing buffalo goat and cow milk in its production.It is rich in protein. Trileche, whose homeland is Mexico and South America usually served with caramel saucer

How Many Calories is 1 Serving of Trileche ?

One serving of trileche prepared with basic ingredients is approximately 165 calories.

Ingredients

For the Cake :
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 packet of vanilla
  • 1 packet of baking powder
For the syrup:
  • 3 cups of cold milk
  • Half a glass of granulated sugar
  • 200 ml cream
For the caramel sauce :
  • 5 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of soft butter
  • 1 packet of cream

Steps

  1. First, separate the yolks and whites of the eggs into different containers.
  2. Whisk the egg whites with salt until they turn into snow. In another bowl, whisk the egg yolks with granulated sugar.
  3. Add the flour, olive oil, vanilla and baking powder into the bowl with the egg yolks and continue whisking.
  4. Gradually add the egg whites to the flour mixture, stirring with a spatula
  5. Pour the mixture on a baking sheet lined with greaseproof paper. Bake in a preheated 170 degree oven for 40-45 minutes.
  6. Put all the ingredients for the syrup in a bowl and whisk until the milk foams well. Put the syrup you have prepared in the refrigerator and let it cool.
  7. For the caramel sauce; Put the sugar in a pan with a non-stick surface and cook until it melts. Add butter and cream and mix quickly.
  8. While the cake is warm, poke holes with a toothpick and pour the cold syrup over it. When the syrup is completely absorbed, add the caramel sauce on top.
Bon Appetit :)
