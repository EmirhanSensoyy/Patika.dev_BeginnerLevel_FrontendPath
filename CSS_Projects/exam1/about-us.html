Who are Kodluyoruz ?

Kodluyoruz, working to train world-class software developers in Turkey is a
social enterprise that aims to make our country a technology development center.


At Kodluyoruz, our main goals are:

  • Considering our values, software developers trained in Turkey on a
    global scale to contribute to the fact that there are software developers who will create value.
  • Starting from high school, to be able to respond to career development needs in the career path.
  • To ensure that the mechanism is established that students can easily use.
